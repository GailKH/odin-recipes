Earl Gray Tea Bread

A light, tender bread to delight tea lovers.

Ingredients:

  • 1 & 1/2 tbsp Earl Gray Tea Leaves
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1 & 1/2 cup Flour
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 cup Butter, softened
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1/2 cup Sour Cream or Plain Greek Yogurt
  • 1 tsp Lemon Peel
  • 2/3 cup Confectioners' Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Water

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Grease and flour a loaf pan. (You may use other pans, adjust the baking time accordingly.)
  3. Beat together the butter and sugar.
  4. Gradually mix in the eggs and vanilla.
  5. Combine the remainder of the dry ingredients.
  6. Gradually mix dry mixture with the butter mixture.
  7. Bake for an hour and twenty-five minutes, or until toothpick inserted in the centre comes out clean.
  8. Cool on a wire rack.
  9. Mix lemon, sugar and water into a bowl until well combined.
  10. Drizzle the lemon mixture over the bread and allow time to set.
