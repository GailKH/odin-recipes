BBQ Tofu

A vegetarian alternative to a classic chicken favourite.

Ingredients:

  • 16oz Package of Extra-Firm Tofu
  • 1 cup Milk or Non-Dairy Alternative
  • 1 Egg or Egg Substitute
  • 1 cup Panko Bread Crumbs
  • 1/2 cup Flour
  • 1 tsp Basil
  • 1/2 tsp Oregano
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 cup Your favourite barbeque sauce

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Prepare a sheet pan with either parchment paper or oil.
  3. Slice tofu into thin strips.
  4. Combine flour and spices in a small bowl.
  5. In a seperate bowl, combine the egg and milk and whisk until mixed well.
  6. Pour out bread crumbs into a third bowl.
  7. Roll the tofu in the flour mixture, brushing off the excess.
  8. Dip the coated tofu into the egg mixture.
  9. Coat the tofu in the panko.
  10. Arrange tofu in a single layer on the sheet pan.
  11. Bake for 20 minutes.
  12. Remove the tofu from the oven and toss with the barbeque sauce of your choice.
  13. Return to the oven and bake for another 20 minutes.
