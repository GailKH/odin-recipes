Chickpea Mango Salad

Ingredients:

  • 14oz Can of Chickpeas
  • 1-2 Mangos
  • 2 Avocados
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 30g Spinach
  • 1 Lemon
  • 3 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps:

  1. Peel, remove the pit, and dice the mango and avocado.
  2. Dice the red onion, tomatoes, and spinach.
  3. Drain and rinse the chickpeas.
  4. Combine the juice of the lemon, olive oil, garlic into a seperate bowl.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Combine the dressing with the salad, toss.
  7. For best taste allow the salad to sit for one hour, or refridgerate overnight.
